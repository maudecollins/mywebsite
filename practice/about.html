My name is Maude Collins and I am an aspiring software engineer. I grew up in Seattle, Washington skiing and hiking with my family. The summer going into 8th grade we moved to New Canaan, CT, and I transitioned from a liberal arts carefree school to an all-girls, intensely catholic, uniform-wearing middle school. I was lucky enough to be exposed to Robotics, and Computer Science early on in my highschool career. During my Senior year I took AP Computer Science and thoroughly enjoyed it, in spite of the late nights of coding. After high school I attended Franklin & Marshall College. I am currently a rising senior with a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics. My hope is to be a software engineer after graduation. However, I strive to eventually go back to school to become an environmental engineer.
